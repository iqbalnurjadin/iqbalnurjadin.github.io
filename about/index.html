Our Story

On September 2018, +Spark Summit engaged over 100 like-minded individuals in a passionate conversation about meaningful, impactful careers. The one-day conference sparked challenging discussions about today’s most pressing social issues, incorporating speakers, career panels, and networking opportunities. Perspectives were transformed, knowledge was shared, and most importantly, +Spark became a hub for young leaders to connect and form a strong community.

The summit unknowingly launched SparkSocialImpact.org. While Adelle was a part of the leadership team that brought +Spark Summit to life, Mari and Iqbal came to the conference as curious attendees. Shocked that the high quality event was organized entirely by students, Mari and Iqbal both deeply identified with the conference’s mission to inspire youth to go beyond themselves by pursuing purpose-driven career pathways. The three of us crossed paths on this day, coming together to jointly see how we could further this goal and reach even more students and young professionals.

The realization of this shared frustration drove us to transform our interests into action by creating this centralized online platform. We are on a mission to lower the barriers to the impact space, demystify the belief that there is a trade-off between purpose and profit, and ultimately spark action among youth to overcome pervasive societal challenges. Our journey is just beginning, and we hope to continue to mobilize young minds to pursue a purpose beyond themselves.

With love & determination,

Adellene Tanuri, Mari Harnish, & Iqbal Nurjadin

Who We Are

Adellene Odelia Tanuri

Growing up in Indonesia, Adelle was constantly confronted with the reality of poverty and inequality in the country she calls home. This early exposure planted the seed of her passion to instigate social change, especially through education and financial inclusion. Her holistic experience in the nonprofit, private, and public sector, gave rise to her ambition to create collaborations across the society in order to catalyze social impact. She believes in the potential of innovative solutions like impact investing, financial technology, and social enterprises – where there is a symbiotic relationship between profit and purpose.

Her ambitions are reflected in her leadership experience founding Spark USA, her past involvement in Microfinance at Berkeley, and past work experiences in the Executive Office of the President of Indonesia and an impact investing fund based in the Bay Area called Just Business.
